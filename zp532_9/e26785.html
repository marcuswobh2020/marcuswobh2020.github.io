DEMI-FINE JEWELLERY CARE

Our 18K gold vermeil and sterling silver jewellery is lovingly handcrafted. To maintain the lustre of your jewellery, please follow our care instructions:

• Avoid wearing your jewellery while swimming, washing up, showering or exercising.

• Avoid contact with chemicals, including fragrance and cosmetics, and abrasive surfaces, taking care to avoid catching on items of clothing.

• Rinse in cold water, pat dry with soft 100% cotton cloth.

The care, wear, and storage of your jewellery influences its appearance; with sterling silver tarnishing and gold vermeil wearing away gradually over time. Sterling silver"s shine can be renewed with a silver polishing cloth, while gold vermeil can be restored to its original brilliance with our dedicated replating service, so you can love your pieces for a lifetime.
